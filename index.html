Case Studies

The Paradox of Choice

Exploring the sweet spot of customer satisfaction and how the number of product options affects both satisfaction ratings and completion rates.

Key finding: 8-12 options delivers peak satisfaction (8.7/10) and completion rates (92%), creating the optimal balance between choice variety and decision paralysis.

Social Media Engagement Decay

Analyzing how engagement with posts declines over time across platforms and identifying the optimal posting strategies for maximum reach and interaction.

Key finding: Platform engagement half-lives vary significantly (Instagram: 21 hours, Twitter: 4 hours, LinkedIn: 48 hours), with content type being a stronger predictor of longevity than posting time.

Cricket Analytics Project

Analyzing how winning the coin toss correlates with match outcomes across different cricket formats and conditions.

Key finding: Subcontinent matches show the strongest correlation (62.1%) between winning the toss and winning the match, suggesting environmental factors significantly influence outcomes in these regions.
